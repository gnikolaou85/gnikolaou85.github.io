Astro Cloud

What is Astro Cloud?

Astro Cloud is a service provided by Astro, which stores your content securely and keeps your apps up to date across all your devices. That means all your stuff — photos, files, notes and more are safe and available wherever you are. Astro Cloud comes with 20 GB of free storage and you can add more storage at any time.

Astro Cloud - Photos

All your photos and videos.
Always available.



  • A lifetime of photos accessible from any device.
    • With Astro Cloud - Photos, you can browse, search, and share all the photos and videos from any of your devices, whether they were taken yesterday or years ago.

  • Take all the photos you want. Without worrying about space on your devices.
    • To save space on your devices, the original, full‑resolution photos you take are automatically uploaded to Astro Cloud - Photos. You’ll still always have a lightweight version of every photo on your device, and you can download the originals whenever you need them.

  • The easiest way to share photos with friends and family.
    • Shared Albums let you create albums for trips, events, or whatever you want. Simply choose the friends and family members who can see, comment, and add their own photos and videos.

Astro Cloud - Drive

Any type of file.
All in one place.



  • All your files available from all your devices.
    • The files you keep safe in Astro Cloud - Drive are easy to get to, no matter which device you’re using. You can access all your files from any device using our login gate for Astro Cloud.

  • Organize files however you want. Get to them on any device you want.
    • Astro Cloud - Drive lets you organize your files with folders, rename them, and tag them with colors. You can even have everything in your Personal Computer or Smartphone automatically available in your Astro Cloud - Drive.

  • Share folders and files with ease.
    • Astro Cloud - Drive makes it fast and easy to work with others from anywhere. Just send a private link and they’ll get instant access to the folders and files you’ve chosen to share. You control whether they can view, share, or edit each file, and you can change these settings at any time.You can also set an access timer, or even schedule the accessibility of a shared folder. And anyone can participate since it works across any device, and the web.
